I am currently a visiting research student at ETH Zürich, on a Fulbright Student Research Fellowship. My work focuses on efficient DRAM design for machine learning applications. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Onur Mutlu this year at ETH Zürich. My research interests lie across security, deep learning, and computer architecture. I spent two years completing my MEng with the Energy Efficient Circuits and Systems group at MIT MTL. Part of my research was also with the Spoken Languages and Systems Group at MIT CSAIL. During my MEng, I was advised by Professor Anantha Chandrakasan and Dr. Jim Glass.

This most recent summer, I had the pleasure of working on NVidia's Autonomous Driving Team to build new versions of PilotNet. In 2017, I worked with the Google Acoustic Modeling research team with Prof. Khe Chai Sim. In prior summers, I worked at Yahoo and Square.

I received my B.S. in Computer Science from MIT in 2016. Andover '13. I like painting and building fast cars.
