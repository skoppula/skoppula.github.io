I am currently a research engineer at Google DeepMind, and part of the PRISM vision research group at University College London. I currently work on dynamic 3D vision, visual representation learning, and video understanding. My research lies across computer vision, natural language understanding, computer security, and computer architecture.

In 2019, I was a visiting researcher at ETH Zürich on a Fulbright Research Fellowship working with Prof. Onur Mutlu. I completed my MEng with the Energy Efficient Circuits and Systems group at MIT MTL in 2018. Part of my research was also with the Spoken Languages and Systems Group at MIT CSAIL. During my MEng, I was advised by Professor Anantha Chandrakasan and Dr. Jim Glass.

In prior years, I had the worked on NVidia's Autonomous Driving Team to build new versions of PilotNet. In 2017, I worked with the Google Acoustic Modeling research team with Prof. Khe Chai Sim. In prior summers, I worked at Yahoo and Square.

I received my B.S. in Computer Science from MIT in 2016. Andover '13. HCSSiM '10. I love painting and building racecars.
